    Redesigning Humber Current Website

    Revamping the Humber Current webpage was a venture into enhancing user experience and fostering seamless connections. Armed with Figma, I meticulously reimagined every facet of the site. Navigational ease took precedence; I streamlined pathways for employers and recruiters to discover student talent effortlessly. Through thoughtful design, I integrated interactive elements that not only showcased student portfolios but also engaged visitors. The revamped site became a hub where innovation met functionality, elevating Humber Current into a dynamic platform, bridging the gap between students and opportunities.
