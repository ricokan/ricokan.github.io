    • Pixels vs Vectors

    Design for a game cover using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator

    In the creation of "Pixels vs Vectors," a captivating game cover art, I seamlessly blended the realms of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. This artwork explores the dynamic interplay between pixels and vectors, showcasing a visually stimulating clash of styles. The meticulous integration of playful imagery and diverse typography not only complements the game's title but also adds depth to its narrative. Through this project, I honed my skills in digital artistry, mastering the art of balancing vibrant visuals and compelling typography to craft an enticing game cover that captivates the audience's imagination.
