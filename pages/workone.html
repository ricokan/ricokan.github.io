    • Say Cheese

    A morning routine poster using Adobe Illustrator

    "Say Cheese" is a vibrant morning event routine poster crafted with Adobe Illustrator, bringing everyday activities to life in a playful cartoon theme. This poster captures the essence of our morning rituals, reminiscent of the act of brushing teeth. Through this artwork, I demonstrated my proficiency in Illustrator, showcasing skills in graphic design and creativity. The design incorporates a thoughtful choice of colors and typography, enhancing the overall visual appeal. With attention to detail, I depicted event-specific information, such as date and time, ensuring a visually engaging and informative poster within a limited space.
