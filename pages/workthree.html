    • Typography Poster (Helvetica)

    Helvetica Typography Poster using Adobe Illustrator

    For the typography poster, I delved into the timeless allure of Helvetica, a fontface revered globally for its clarity and elegance. Embracing its legacy, I crafted a poster that pays homage to this iconic typeface. Infused with a warm color palette dominated by vibrant oranges, the poster resonates with creativity and energy. The focal point became the font's inception year, magnificently emphasized, underscoring its historical significance. Additionally, the poster showcases various Helvetica variants, offering viewers a comprehensive visual journey through this font's versatility and enduring charm. Through this project, I celebrated Helvetica's legacy while exploring innovative design techniques.
